Inspection Process

Returns come in in daily. We receive packages from USPS, FedEx and UPS and they are typically received inside the big green gaylord at the third dock door. You can pick these packages up using a cart or buggy and bring them to the returns area in the back of the warehouse. During peak times these packages will need to be seperated by date of arrival.

Carefully open packages using scissors or box cutters. Be very careful not to accidentally damage items inside packages when opening. It's best to make a small opening and carefully make wider.

Clean Up

Clean up as you go to ensure we are keeping a clutter free and safe working environment. Boxes should be cut with box cutter and disposed of in red bins. Once the red bins are full empty contents in dumpster.

Throw away plastic trash into black garbage bins. Once the trash is full please take out bag and throw away in dumpster.

Inventory Process

After returns are inspected and their condition is manually documented, they are entered into a spreadsheet and received into Netsuite.

Put Away

The final step in the returns process is the putting items back on the shelves. In order to do so you will be given a put away sheet which will show you the location of each item inside the warehouse
